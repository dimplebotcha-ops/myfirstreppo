My Trip to Vanjangi

Last Winter, I visited Vanjangi — a peaceful hilltop destination with beautiful sunrise views. The early morning trek was a bit challenging, but the scenery at the top made it all worth it. Watching the sun rise above the clouds was a magical moment I’ll never forget!

Travel Tips

  • Start your trek early (around 4 AM)
  • Carry a flashlight or headlamp
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Bring water and light snacks
